Key Trade-offs

  • Simplicity vs Capability: Arduino is simpler, Pi is more capable
  • Power vs Performance: Arduino uses less power, Pi offers higher performance
  • Real-time vs Multitasking: Arduino excels at timing, Pi excels at multitasking
